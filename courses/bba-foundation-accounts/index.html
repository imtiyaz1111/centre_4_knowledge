About BBA / Foundation Accounts

The BBA / Foundation Accounts course at Centre 4 Knowledge is specially designed for students who want to build a strong base in accounting before moving to advanced commerce and management studies.

This course focuses on developing a clear understanding of core accounting principles such as journal entries, ledger posting, trial balance, and basic financial statements.

Classes are conducted through live interactive online sessions where students can ask questions, solve practical problems, and gain clarity in real time.

The teaching approach emphasizes logical explanation, practical illustrations, and step-by-step problem solving—ensuring students gain confidence in accounts from the very beginning.

This course is ideal for students planning to pursue BBA, B.Com, CA Foundation, CS Foundation, or other commerce-related programs.
