About ICSE / IGCSE Accounts

The ICSE / IGCSE Accounts course at Centre 4 Knowledge is specially designed for school students who need strong conceptual understanding and structured exam preparation.

Our teaching focuses on core topics such as journal entries, ledger accounts, trial balance, final accounts, and practical problem-solving aligned strictly with the ICSE and IGCSE syllabus.

Classes are conducted through live interactive online sessions, ensuring students can ask doubts freely and understand each concept in depth.

Special emphasis is placed on presentation skills, accuracy, and exam-writing techniques required to score well in board examinations.

This course builds a solid foundation for higher commerce studies, including Class 11 & 12 Accounts, B.Com, BBA, and professional courses.
